the golden record

a 12-inch gold-plated copper disc — a message in a bottle, thrown into an ocean a billion years wide, for whoever finds the ship.

  • 115 images of earth encoded in its grooves
  • greetings in 55 human languages — and one from the humpback whales
  • 90 minutes of music: bach, gamelan, blind willie johnson, chuck berry
  • the sounds of surf, wind, a heartbeat, a kiss, a mother's first words
  • a map of 14 pulsars that points back to our sun
  • a stylus is included, with playing instructions etched on the cover
  • expected to remain playable for over a billion years
